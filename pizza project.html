Margarita Pizza

A bubbly crust, crushed San Marzano tomato sauce, fresh mozzarella and basil, a drizzle of olive oil, and a sprinkle of salt.

Buffalo Chicken Pizza

Juicy shredded chicken is the star of the show. It's bathed in a tangy, creamy mixture of ranch dressing and buffalo sauce and placed on a pizza crust along with lots of mozzarella cheese, blue cheese, and thinly sliced red onions.

California Pizza

A thin-crust pizza noted for its fresh, nontraditional toppings, such as chicken, peanut sauce, artichoke hearts, and goat cheese rather than the standard pepperoni and mozzarella.

Sicilian Pizza

Sicilian pizza is a style of pizza originating from Sicily, characterized by its thick, rectangular crust and often baked in a sheet pan
